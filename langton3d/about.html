Langton 3D
A 3D Langton’s Ant / Turmite playground
Open Simulator Source

What this is

This experiment renders an infinite-looking voxel trail in A-Frame while one or more ants (turmites) walk a shared grid. You can spawn ants, choose palettes, schedule spawns, and share the whole setup via a URL.

Controls

  • Move camera: WASD/Arrows + mouse look
  • Pause/Play: Space
  • Single step: N
  • Reset: R
  • Help: H or ?

Sharing

  • Share copies a preset URL to your clipboard.
  • Share/Import opens a drawer panel with the URL field, import, and presets.
  • Snapshot downloads/shares an image and also copies the preset URL.

State + color model (multi-ant)

Each cell stores a shared numeric stateIndex (drives behavior) and a hex color (visual only). Ants interact through stateIndex. Each ant’s palette controls only the colors it paints.
